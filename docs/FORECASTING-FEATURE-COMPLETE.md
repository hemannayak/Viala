## ✅ What We Built

### **1. Advanced Forecasting Engine (`lib/forecasting.ts`)**
- **Moving Average Model**: 3-month historical sales analysis
- **Seasonal Multipliers**: Dynamic demand adjustments for different seasons
- **Medicine-Specific Logic**: Special handling for ORS, Paracetamol, etc.
- **Risk Assessment**: Automatic categorization (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH/CRITICAL)
- **Revenue Impact**: Calculates potential losses from stockouts

### **2. Seasonal Mode Intelligence**
- **Normal Mode**: Standard demand patterns (1.0x multiplier)
- **Monsoon Mode**: 2.5x for ORS & Paracetamol, 3.2x for Cough/Cold medicines
- **Summer Mode**: 3.5x for Hydration, 2.2x for Antihistamines
- **Winter Mode**: 2.8x for Cough/Cold, 2.2x for Analgesics
